Sleep Hours vs Exam Scores Analysis

Intuitively, you might expect that students who sleep more perform better on their exams. However, the data is telling us a different story. The scatterplot below plots every student’s sleep hours and their exam score. The ability to filter for the student’s distance to their school is also added to the plot. The flat red trend line confirms there is little to no correlation between how a student’s sleep hours and how well they do on their exams. This tells us that sleep alone is not a meaningful predictor of academic performance.
