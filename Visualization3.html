Sleep Hours vs Test Scores Analysis

Intuitively, you might expect that students who sleep more perform better on their exams. However, the data is telling us a different story. The scatterplot below plots every student’s sleep hours and their exam score. The flat orange trend line confirms there is little to no correlation between how a student’s sleep hours and how well they do on their exams. This tells us that sleep alone is not a meaningful predictor of academic performance.
